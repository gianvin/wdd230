Bulacan Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The Bulacan Chamber of Commerce aims to establish Local Business Councils in every city and municipality of Bulacan; and maintain a mutually beneficial relationship with the local business councils. It helps Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises to promote and strengthen their business. They work for strong public and private partnership for economic progess.
